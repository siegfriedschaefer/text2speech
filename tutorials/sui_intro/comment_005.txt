After that the usual 12 word recovery phrase is created by the Wallet.
This recovery phrase is the key to all your accounts you generate later on,
so write them down on two papers and put them at two different save places.

Trust me, you have to do this or you will loose access to your assets at some
time in the future, because you will change your browser, mobile or notebook in the future
and won't have access to the passphrase if you just store it on your device.

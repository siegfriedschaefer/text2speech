This tutorial shows you how to build a Metaverse Dapp.
It shows you all the steps needed to offer a virtual Swabian Pretzel to your Dapp users.
The Pretzel is offered to your users via a Pretzel Vending Machine.
The Vending Machine's business logic is implemented as a Smart Contract in Solidity,
it's Augmented Reality representation runs in a WebXR environment implemented by Babylon js.

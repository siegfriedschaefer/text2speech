In the Golem Network compute nodes can participate as a provider node or a requestor node.

The default implementation of the service is called YAGNA.

A Provider shares its unused compute resources like CPU, GPU, memory and storage for Requestors in exchange of the utility token GLM.

The micropayment is most often executed through the Polygon network.

The mainnet is used for normal paid operations, Ethereums Holesky testnet is used for development.
